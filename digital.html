Digital Awareness

Essential tips and quick facts to help young people use social media safely and smartly.

Privacy

Use private profiles, review permissions and enable two-factor authentication.

Citation: Common digital safety guidelines (WHO, 2023).

Verify

Check the source before sharing — look for credible accounts and verify images.

Citation: Media literacy research (Smith & Lee, 2022).

Balance

Set screen-time routines, sleep-friendly habits, and real-world time with friends.

Citation: Youth wellbeing studies (Adams et al., 2021).
